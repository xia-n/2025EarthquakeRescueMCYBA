About Us

Donation Appeal

To All Social Organizations, Compassionate Enterprises, and Kind-Hearted Individuals,

At approximately 12:50 PM on March 28, 2025, a 7.9-magnitude earthquake struck Myanmar, with a depth of 30 kilometers. The powerful quake has caused widespread devastation across Sagaing, Mandalay, Magway, northeastern Shan State, and Naypyidaw, leaving countless families displaced and communities in urgent need of aid.

In response to this tragic disaster, the Myanmar Chinese Youth Business Association has launched an emergency fundraising initiative: "Together in Love and Earthquake Relief." We are calling on all businesses, organizations, charitable institutions, and individuals to stand with us and extend a helping hand to those affected.

The scale of this disaster is immense. Thousands have lost their homes, schools have been destroyed, and many are left without access to food, clean water, and medical care. In these critical moments, your generosity can bring hope and relief to the people who need it the most.

We encourage both companies and individuals to reach out to the Myanmar Chinese Youth Business Association to coordinate donations. We assure you that every contribution will be managed with full transparency, in compliance with national laws and regulations, and under the supervision of the public. Every single donation—no matter the amount—will be used effectively to provide urgent relief and long-term support for the victims.

In times of crisis, compassion and unity are our greatest strengths. Your kindness and generosity can make a life-changing difference for the earthquake survivors. Let us come together, hand in hand, to bring hope, comfort, and strength to those who have lost so much. Together, we can help rebuild lives and create a brighter future.

With our deepest gratitude,
Myanmar Chinese Youth Business Association
April 1, 2025
