About Us

The Myanmar Chinese Youth Business Association (MCYBA) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering young entrepreneurs, fostering business development, and supporting communities in times of crisis.

Our Mission

  • Disaster Relief & Humanitarian Aid: Supporting earthquake victims, fundraising, and rebuilding efforts.
  • Youth Entrepreneurship & Business Development: Providing training, mentorship, and funding for young entrepreneurs.
  • Community Support & Education: Scholarships, vocational training, and financial literacy programs.

Our Commitment

MCYBA operates with transparency, accountability, and dedication to the people we serve. Every initiative is conducted ethically and in compliance with national regulations, ensuring that resources are used effectively.

Together, we can create opportunities, support those in need, and inspire future generations. Join us in building a stronger, more resilient Myanmar.
